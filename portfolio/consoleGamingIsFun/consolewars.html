What is A Console War

Well, console wars are a long-running battle for supremacy between Xbox and PlayStation owners. This seems trivial but it the argument can get intense really quick between the owners of either console.

What Do They Fight Over?

Some of the topics they fight over are:

  • Graphics
  • Frame rate
  • Console specific games

How I Feel

The war between consoles has always made me laugh. The way I look at it is they are both great consoles and when you are playing one or the other you don't see any difference. So my philosophy has always been to choose the one that the majority of your friends play on. Doing this will give you the most happiness with your purchase.
