Xbox One

The Xbox One is the Latest gaming console developed by Microsoft. It is from the 8th Generation of gaming consoles. When it was originally announced Microsoft planned to make the games non sharable. If they were to do this it would keep gamers from sharing games with each other or trading them into companies like Gamestop. The announcement of this enraged many gamers and Microsoft quickly got rid of this feature. This console featured an innovated idea by adding the ability to connect your cable tv through the xbox. Doing this allowed you to control your cable through your xbox by either using the Kinect sensor or your controller. Also new to the Xbox one was a Bluray disk drive, before this was only available through the Playstation 3 or buying a Bluray player. The Kinect sensor adds a different dynamic to the gaming experience, similar to that of the Wii. The difference is with Kinect, your body is the controller. The kinect sensor was introduced to Xbox with the Xbox 360. However, it was completely redone for the Xbox One and operates a lot more flawlessly.
